NBA Team Wins Analysis

Team Wins Comparison

The purpose of this project was to analyze the performance of all of the teams in the NBA in order to gain a better understanding of overall team performance and consistency of performance from the standpoint of how frequently the team won a game.

In order to accomplish this, data on the number of wins for each of the 30 teams in the NBA was analyzed to assess and compare each team's performance in terms of total wins, consistency of wins over a 5 year period of time, mean wins of the top 5 performing teams vs. the worst and median performing teams, and an analysis on quartiles across the NBA.

The data for this analysis was based on the last 5 years (2015 - 2019) of data obtained from Basketball-reference.com. The data was imported into pandas and plotted using the matplotlib module.

This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis as well as explanations and descriptions of the results of the analysis.
