Team Wins Comparison

A barchart of the total wins for each team in the NBA was plotted to establisg a clear comparison of the level of success for each team over the past 5 years, in terms of the total number of wins. The chart revealed a wide range of wins across all 300 teams, with the top performing team a full 196 wins ahead of the worst performing team.
